Magno Cerqueira

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Sports Nutritionist | MSc in Food, Nutrition & Health

Magno is a certified nutritionist with a Master’s degree in Food, Nutrition, and Health, specialized in the physiological foundations of personalized training, sports nutrition, and advanced medicine. He combines scientific depth with real-world athletic application, delivering performance-based nutritional strategies tailored to each individual.

His approach integrates clinical knowledge with high-performance sport demands — ensuring every athlete receives a data-driven, individualized strategy.

  • Personalized Consultations
  • Physical & Dietary Assessment
  • Customized Meal Plan Development
  • Performance-Oriented Nutritional Strategy
  • Functional Nutrition Approach
  • Specialist in Athletic Performance Development
